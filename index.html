Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computer vision. Some of the areas I am currently working on are: Domain adaptation, in particular between synthetic and real datasets; GANs; Depth, and Normal estimation; Inverse Rendering; Video classification; and Inpainting. I am looking to further explore new research areas in computer vision.
